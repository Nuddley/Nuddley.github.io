History:

1993:

A young car enthusiest Chris Jones started a little customs shop in queenstown. He hired some old friends and they mostly just helped fix cars. But in the weekends, when the customers went home Chris modified his own car a 1990 Nissan R34. He turned this car into a beast and drove it to track days and races winning many times over.


1995:

The local car club had gained popularity and Chris began having other car enthusiests come to him through the club to get their cars race ready. Chris and the team got them all race ready and helped start many racing careers for drivers, mechanics, and principles alike.

Racing:

1997:

Chris moved his sights to rally, and enroled a team in the C class 1997 New Zealand rally. His car a Toyota corolla hatchback which he converted to all wheel drive, a bigger engine, and a full roll cage in under three weeks to be ready for the rally sprint. The team took third place with Chris at the wheel and May as his co driver. They would continue in C class rallying until 2001 when they started a new championship.


2001:

Chris and May took the company into the GT5 championship, their driver Liam Lawson(Now an F2 driver) taking the car to championship standards and two years after "QT customs racing" moved to GT4 where they raced for seven years before finally making the move to GT3 in 2010 where they raced another seven years and finally in 2017 they made the move to GT2 where we remain to this day. Our driver Ben Engelsen taking multipull championship wins so far and many runner up positions.
