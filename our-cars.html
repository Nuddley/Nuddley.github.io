RX8 1997


This car is lowered with Comprelli coilovers, has a supercharged v8 from a hellcat and has a full custom bodykit.


We bought the car as a bare chassis with plenty of rust damage. We fixed the rust, added our new body panels, put the biggest engine we could fit in it, and gave it a set of five spoke bronze kominos wrapped in the finest perrelli rubber.


The car did numerous track days and club meetings and is a sort of right of passage to new employees to set a lap time in it. Its been to drift events in teretonga, and has been taken to SEMA and won the annual award for best bodykit in the whole show. The car participated in a road cruise from bluff along the east coast of new zealand to the tip of the north island, and then back down along the west coast. After some much needed maintanence from the journey it was taken to another event almost immediatly. The rx8 is a real trooper earning the nickname, "The Soldier"
